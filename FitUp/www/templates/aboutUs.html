Nowadays, more people
have given their interests on health for somereason.
Although gyms or decent sports places are pricey, people still invest their money in it.
From this point, it helps health businesses significantly increased within a short time.
However, there are people who do not have sufficient time and money to do so.
For this reason, they would have nothing to encourage them to exercise.
Otherwise, they would have to search information by themselves.


Regarding the people who push themselves to exercise on their own,
there are plenty of accessible information and resources provided online.
Our project, Fit-Up application, consists of valuable information and suggestion
such as advice articles and video courses provided from specialists and trainers to help people gain the correct healthy routine as desire.


Fit-Up application is available as both the Web and Hybrid application.
We allow people to explore fitness courses and purchase breathtaking videos that fit themselves.
On the other hand, we also let trainers upload information and videos to the platform.
